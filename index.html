August 2010

Ben applies to jobs in Chicago and New York, planning to move wherever he gets a job first. The New York Times calls, and he moves to New York.

May 2013

Cola turns down a job at a design agency in Boston in order to move to New York - with no plan on getting or finding a job once she gets there.

Any of Ben’s relationships end up only lasting about 3 months, so he just wasn’t that interested in dating.

Cola is frustrated with “Let’s meet at a bar” being considered a date, she gives up on dating, and instead decides to go adventuring as often as possible.

In December, 2014

Fate and a lot of snow brought Ben and Cola together. They met each other on a snowboarding meetup trip in Killington, VT. They were assigned the same cabin, where these two strangers found out they were actually neighbors back in Brooklyn.

3 days later, Cola asked Ben out to dinner. He took her out for Falafel. They bond over their similar love of music, and the shared yearning to someday see Bonobo live in concert.

One day, Cola soldered some pieces onto Ben’s quadcopter. That’s when he knew the search was over.

One night, Ben made Cola tikka masala from scratch for dinner. That’s when she knew he was the one.

Ben asked Cola to move into his place, she was already spending most of her time there anyways! They spent an afternoon moving her stuff in from across the street.

April 2015

Ben’s Mom and brother Tom visit Brooklyn and they spend all afternoon walking through Brooklyn Botanic Garden.

May 2015

Cola goes caving with Ben, he’s amazed she’s willing to spend the day crawling through a dark, muddy hole in the ground together.

They visit Ben’s friends Steve and Amy on the weekends to hang out, but end up spending most of the time flying quadcopters in the back yard with their kids.

They take their first true vacation together – a two and a half week trip to visit Cola's sister Pooja in Hong Kong, and tour Japan with Ben's friends.

November 2015

Ben goes to Cola's parent's house in Boston for Christmas, he meets her sister Salome for the first time.

They move into a bigger apartment, literally right next door, and they painted the walls bright colors to make it feel more like a home.

December 2016

They had always dreamed of getting a dog together, and planned to adopt that spring. But fate stepped in once again, and a friend they had dog-sat for in the past, called saying they just couldn’t take care of their puppy anymore and were looking for a good home for her. Ben & Cola already loved this puppy, so a few weeks later, added the newest addition to their adventuring family. They named her Chihiro after one of their favorite movie characters.

They’ve now made the trip on which they met into an annual tradition! Each winter they go snowboarding together almost every weekend. Even with a new puppy, they always make time for annual trips to BC, Canada to ride Whistler Blackcomb.

They first bonded over wanting to see Bonobo live in concert, and they finally saw him play right in their adopted home of Brooklyn!

To keep up the music streak, they visit Ben’s friends Paul and Krystle in DC to go to several concerts and festivals. Chihiro’s first time in our country’s capital!

It becomes apparent that they want to get married, and start planning what spending the rest of their lives together would look like.

Cola’s friend Becky invites them to Boston to make an *amazing* brunch. Chihiro doesn’t even look at the plate of bacon placed right in front of her!

Chihiro takes them hiking on Lake George, to a family cabin where Cola spent her childhood summers.

May 2017

On the weekends that they don’t go to Lake George, Chihiro takes them to Prospect Park in Brooklyn, where she can run free with literally hundreds of other dogs.

They attend Ben’s cousin, Seth and Melissa’s beautiful wedding in Chicago, where they reunite with Ben’s family and make some new friends.

Ben starts sending Cola syllabi for user experience design programs – she’d been talking about it for long enough!

While swimming in Lake George, they relax on a floating dock and Ben proposes to Cola and makes it official – they had been talking about it long enough! (I think it’s cute and funny to have the two “long enough” statements after each other, personally, but I’d also understand it you wanna leave it out)

August 2017

They take Chihiro to Prince Edward Island, Canada, for two and a half weeks with Cola's parents, their friend Casey, and her dog Coco. Chihiro has so much fun, she sleeps for an entire week upon returning to Brooklyn!

Once a month, they host a homemade Pizza Night at their apartment to keep in touch with friends, even though they all have busy schedules now.

They’ve traveled to 3 Continents, 6 Countries, 7 Snowboarding Mountains, 13 States - and 2 Provinces, and 14 Cities since they started dating, and they’ve just barely gotten started.

Now

Whether it's a 15 hour flight to Japan, or a 15 minute walk to Prospect Park, Ben and Cola love to share amazing adventures.

Now they’re excited to start their next adventure together, and they hope you can join them as they embark on their next journey
